Privacy Policy for Disciple Game

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

ZitoCreates (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the mobile game Disciple Game (the “Game”), currently available on Android devices.

The Game is free to play and supported by advertising. Users may optionally make a one-time in-app purchase to remove ads or unlock premium features.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Game.

By using the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We do not directly collect personally identifiable information (such as your name or email) unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, when contacting support).

b. Device and Usage Data

  • Device type, model, and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Gameplay data (progress, session duration, interactions)

c. In-App Purchase Information

Transactions are processed securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store payment details. We may receive purchase status and product identifiers.

3. Third-Party Services

a. AdMob (Google Ads)

We use AdMob to display advertisements in the Game. AdMob may collect Advertising ID, device information, IP address, and ad interaction data.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

b. LevelPlay (ironSource Mediation)

We use LevelPlay (ironSource) to manage and optimize ad delivery. ironSource may collect device identifiers, IP address, and ad performance data.

Learn more: ironSource Privacy Policy

c. Google Play Services

Used for in-app purchases and app functionality. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

4. Advertising and Monetization

The Game is monetized through advertisements (including interstitial and rewarded ads) and optional one-time in-app purchases.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your Android device settings or resetting your Advertising ID.

The Game may offer rewarded ads, where users receive in-game rewards in exchange for watching advertisements.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the Game
  • Enable in-app purchases
  • Display and optimize advertisements
  • Improve gameplay experience and performance
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers, to comply with legal obligations, or to protect our rights and users.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the Game, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

8. Your Privacy Rights (LGPD & GDPR Principles)

Depending on your location, including Brazil under LGPD, you have rights to request access, correction, or deletion of your data, and information about how it is used.

9. Children’s Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under 13. If we become aware of personal data collected from children without consent, we will delete it.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your own, where data protection laws may differ.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.
